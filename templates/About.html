About

Diet care is an application that takes user's daily diet and analyses nutritions present in their diet. By means, if user if found to consume excess of unnecessary nutrition / nutrition that is not supposed to take, user can analyse from this application.

This application also presents another segment, that takes daily health issues/ illness that user faces and tries to identify the deficiency of nutrition behind it to get it cure. It also present top 10 food that user must take to cure that deficiency along with weight of that portion present in that food.

This application is made for the course Open Source Systems(15Z010) as a mini-project.

This application's front-end was developed using HTML,CSS and Bootstrop-4. The backend was python(flask) and database used was MySQL.

Impact of COVID-19 has changed the life style of everyone around world. With rise in WFH's and stresses, people seems not to care about their health. As illustrted by the report. This application tend to remind the users diet and get insight from daily diet to improve their health.

The information about these diet was taken from Indian Food Composition Tables writtern by T.Longvah, R.Ananth, K.Bhaskarachary, K.Venkaiah. This book was © 2017 by National Institure of Nutrition, Indian Council of Medical Research, Department of health reasearch, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India.
