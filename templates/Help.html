How to use "Diet Analysis" module
  1. Once after login, select Diet analysis from home page
  2. Once entered inside Diet analysis page, select the food from list of foods that you eat everyday
  3. Once done, finally submit it
  4. Finally, you'll get amount of nutrients present in foods that you eat everyday
  5. Make wise choice in diet after seeing the result!
How to use "Disease & Deficiency" module
  1. Once after login, select Disease & Deficiency from home page
  2. Once entered inside Disease & Deficiency page, select the problems that you have as labeled by body parts
  3. Once done, finally submit it
  4. Finally, you'll get what deficiency causes that problem and what are foods to take to tackle that issue
  5. Make wise choice in diet after seeing the result!
